Rooted in Experience – Growing Through Generations

Broom Forestry and Arb is a family-run forestry company and arboricultural contractor, based at the Broom Farm Estate in Annan, serving South West Scotland and Northern Cumbria. With 20 years of hands-on experience across three generations, we're proud to offer safe, professional, and cost-effective tree and land management solutions.

We operate our own sawmill and firewood production yard, allowing us to provide full-cycle services—from woodland management and windblown clearance to custom timber production. We're fully qualified with NPTC, City & Guilds, and LANTRA certifications, and carry £5 million public liability and £10 million employers liability insurance.

Our Services

Tree Surgery & Arboriculture

  • Tree Felling – Safe and efficient removal of trees of all sizes.
  • Tree Dismantling – Sectional dismantling of trees in confined or sensitive areas.
  • Pruning – Including crown reductions, thinning, and deadwood removal to maintain tree health.
  • Stump Grinding – Fast and tidy stump removal and site clearance.
  • 24/7 Emergency Call-Outs – Storm damage? Dangerous trees? We're on call any time, day or night.

Forestry Services

  • Low-Impact Forestry & Woodland Management
  • Windblown Tree Clearance
  • Small-Scale Commercial Sites – Ideal for jobs others won't take.
  • Land & Vegetation Clearance – Using our excavator with mulching flail.
